Movies

Movies and shows that have inspired and entertained me.

Little Forest (2018)

A young woman grows tired of life in the city and returns to her hometown in the countryside.

The Wind Rises (2013)

This is one of the best love stories i have ever watched and i doubt any story can beat it. The ending is tragic makes it a masterpiece.

Life is Beautiful (1997)

When an open-minded Jewish waiter and his son become victims of the Holocaust, he uses a perfect mixture of will, humor and imagination to protect his son.

Fight Club (1999)

Fight Club is a nearly perfect movie. The filmmaking is actually extraordinary in how lively and dynamic it is from start to finish.

Better Call Saul (2015)

The winner takes it all. But at what cost.

The Mind Explained (2019)

From dreaming to anxiety disorders, exploring what's happening inside the brain.
